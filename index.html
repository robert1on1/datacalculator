Usage Tips

*Opera Mini which mostly opens up the Mobile version of websites. Mobile versions of websites are small in size hence take up very little of the data bundle. This saves data for later use and prolongs your usage period

*After completing your torrent download ensure to stop it from seeding to avoid other people from downloading from your computer or device using up your bundle.

*Your computer may be infected with a virus. A virus will often send out mass emails from your computer without your knowledge.

*Ensure that any software updates like windows updates are always disabled

*Always ensure that you change your wireless password at least once a month or when you think it has been accessed by an unauthorized user
